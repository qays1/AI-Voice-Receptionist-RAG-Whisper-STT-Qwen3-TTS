# BrightMart Store — Policies

## Return Policy (Standard Items)
- Returns accepted within 14 days of purchase.
- Item must be unused and in original packaging.
- Receipt or proof of purchase is required.
- Refund method:
  - Card purchases refunded to the same card within 7–14 business days.
  - Cash purchases refunded in cash or store credit (based on manager approval).

## Return Policy (Electronics)
- Electronics can be returned within 7 days only if:
  - The device is unopened OR
  - The device is defective and confirmed by the electronics desk.
- Opened electronics that are not defective are not eligible for return.
- Some items are final sale (see below).

## Final Sale (Non-Returnable Items)
- Opened earphones/headphones
- Personal hygiene items (e.g., shavers, toothbrushes)
- Clearance items marked “Final Sale”
- Gift cards

## Exchanges
- Exchanges are possible within the same return window.
- If the replacement item has a higher price, the customer pays the difference.

## Warranty (Electronics)
- Standard warranty: 12 months for eligible devices.
- Warranty claims require:
  - Receipt
  - Serial number
  - Original accessories (if requested)
- Warranty processing time: typically 7–21 business days.

## Delivery Policy
- Same-day delivery available for orders placed before 3:00 PM (Amman only).
- Standard delivery (outside Amman): 1–3 business days.
- Delivery fees:
  - Amman: 2 JOD
  - Outside Amman: 4 JOD
- Free delivery for orders above 50 JOD.

## Click & Collect
- Available at both branches.
- Pickup window: within 3 days of confirmation.
- Customer must bring:
  - Order number
  - National ID or passport

## Complaints & Escalation
- If the issue is urgent (wrong delivery, missing items, damaged items):
  - Escalate to store supervisor and request the customer’s order number.
- If the customer requests a manager:
  - Offer a callback within 30 minutes during working hours.
- If the customer is angry or distressed:
  - Apologize, confirm details, and offer escalation or callback.
