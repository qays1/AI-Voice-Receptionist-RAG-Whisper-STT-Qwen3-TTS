# BrightMart Store — FAQ

## General
Q: Do you have parking?
A: Yes. Abdoun branch has street parking nearby. Sweifieh branch has a paid parking facility next to the complex.

Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?
A: Yes. Gift wrapping is available in-store for 1 JOD per item.

Q: Can I pay with Apple Pay?
A: Yes, Apple Pay is accepted at both branches.

## Orders
Q: How can I track my online order?
A: Customers can track orders using the order number through WhatsApp support or by calling customer support.

Q: What if my delivery is late?
A: Ask for the order number and escalate to the delivery team. Offer a callback within 30 minutes during working hours.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
A: Address changes are possible if the order has not been dispatched. Ask for the order number and escalate.

## Products
Q: Do you sell iPhones?
A: Yes, BrightMart sells iPhones depending on stock availability. Ask for the model and storage size.

Q: Do you sell PlayStation 5?
A: Yes, depending on availability. Ask if the customer wants the standard or digital edition.

Q: Do you sell school stationery?
A: Yes. Stationery is available in-store and online.

## Pricing Examples (For Testing)
- iPhone 15 (128GB): 699 JOD (subject to availability)
- PlayStation 5 Standard: 499 JOD (subject to availability)
- Air Fryer (Brand X): 59 JOD

## Escalation Examples
Q: My order arrived damaged. What do I do?
A: Apologize, ask for the order number, and escalate to the supervisor. Offer a callback within 30 minutes.

Q: I want to speak to a manager now.
A: Offer a callback within 30 minutes during working hours, and confirm the customer’s name and phone number.
